## Itinerary
**Trip Overview:**
Our 7-day journey will take us to Japan, exploring the vibrant cities, rich history, and unique culture. We will experience the best of Japan's hospitality, cuisine, and entertainment, making this trip an unforgettable experience.

**Flights:**
To travel to Japan under ₹2L, we recommend booking a flight with a reliable airline such as AirAsia, China Eastern, or Singapore Airlines. We can fly from Kolkata to Osaka or Tokyo with a layover in a city like Kuala Lumpur or Singapore.

**Hotel Accommodations:**
We suggest staying at a hostel or budget hotel in Japan, such as:

* RoheN HAKONEYUMOTO in Hakone (approximately ₹2,500 per night)
* Quality Hostel K's House Tokyo Oasis (approximately ₹2,000 per night)
* Shinagawa-shuku Guest House & Capsule Hotel (approximately ₹1,500 per night)

**Itinerary:**

**Day 1: Arrival in Osaka**

* Arrive at Kansai International Airport
* Check-in at our hostel and spend the day exploring the city
* Visit the famous Dotonbori area and try some local street food

**Day 2: Osaka Sightseeing**

* Start the day with a delicious breakfast at a local café
* Visit the Osaka Castle, a beautiful park with historic monuments
* Explore the Umeda Sky Building, which offers stunning views of the city
* Enjoy a traditional Osaka dinner at a local restaurant

**Day 3: Day Trip to Kyoto**

* Take a day trip to Kyoto, a city famous for its temples and gardens
* Visit the Fushimi Inari Shrine, famous for its thousands of vermillion torii gates
* Explore the Kinkaku-ji Temple, a beautiful golden pavilion
* Return to Osaka in the evening and enjoy a relaxing dinner

**Day 4: Travel to Tokyo**

* Take a bullet train from Osaka to Tokyo
* Check-in at our hostel and spend the day exploring the city
* Visit the famous Shibuya Crossing and take a walk around the trendy Shibuya area

**Day 5: Tokyo Sightseeing**

* Start the day with a delicious breakfast at a local café
* Visit the Meiji Shrine, a beautiful Shinto shrine located in a peaceful forested area
* Explore the Asakusa district, one of the oldest areas in Tokyo
* Enjoy a traditional Tokyo dinner at a local restaurant

**Day 6: Tokyo Sightseeing**

* Visit the Tsukiji Outer Market, a famous fish market with fresh sushi and seafood
* Take a stroll in the beautiful Imperial Palace East Garden
* Explore the Akihabara district, Tokyo's electronic and anime hub
* Enjoy a farewell dinner at a local restaurant

**Day 7: Departure from Tokyo**

* Spend the morning at leisure or visit any last-minute attractions
* Check-out of the hostel and depart for the airport
* Take a flight back to Kolkata, bringing back memories of our wonderful trip to Japan

**Budget Breakdown:**

* Flights: approximately ₹80,000 - ₹1,20,000 (depending on the airline and time of booking)
* Hotel Accommodations: approximately ₹20,000 - ₹30,000 (depending on the hostel and time of booking)
