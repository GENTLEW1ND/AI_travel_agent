**Day 1: Arrival in Imphal, Manipur**

* **Morning:** Arrive at Imphal International Airport (9:00 am)
* **Check-in:** Hotel Imphal (10:00 am) - a comfortable and affordable option near the city center
* **Afternoon:** Visit the Kangla Fort (1:00 pm - 3:00 pm), a historic site that served as the palace of the Manipur rulers
* **Evening:** Explore the local market, especially the Ima Keithel (Mother's Market) (5:00 pm - 7:00 pm), which is run entirely by women

**Day 2: Imphal**

* **Morning:** Visit the Loktak Lake (9:00 am - 11:00 am), the largest freshwater lake in Northeast India, known for its floating islands (phumdis)
* **Boat Ride:** Take a boat ride to the Sendra Island (11:00 am - 1:00 pm) for a panoramic view of the lake
* **Afternoon:** Visit the Keibul Lamjao National Park (2:00 pm - 4:00 pm), the only floating national park in the world, located on the Loktak Lake
* **Evening:** Enjoy local cuisine for dinner at a traditional restaurant (7:00 pm - 9:00 pm)

**Day 3: Imphal to Churachandpur**

* **Morning:** Drive to Churachandpur (8:00 am - 10:00 am), about 60 km from Imphal
* **Afternoon:** Explore the town, visit the local market, and see the scenic views (11:00 am - 2:00 pm)
* **Evening:** Stay overnight at a local hotel in Churachandpur (5:00 pm - onwards)

	+ Hotel Venus
	+ Eikhoigi Hotel
	+ Hotel Eden

**Day 4: Churachandpur to Ukhrul**

* **Morning:** Drive to Ukhrul (8:00 am - 10:00 am), about 120 km from Churachandpur
* **Stop:** Visit the Khayang Peak (10:00 am - 11:00 am) for a breathtaking view
* **Afternoon:** Visit the Shirui Lily Park (1:00 pm - 3:00 pm), home to the rare and endangered Shirui Lily
* **Evening:** Stay overnight at a local guesthouse in Ukhrul (5:00 pm - onwards)

**Day 5: Ukhrul**

* **Morning:** Explore the village of Ukhrul (9:00 am - 11:00 am)
* **Visit:** Hundung Mangva Cave (11:00 am - 1:00 pm), a limestone cave
* **Trek:** Trek to the Khangkhui Cave (2:00 pm - 4:00 pm), known for its stunning natural beauty and historical significance
* **Evening:** Enjoy the local Tangkhul cuisine (7:00 pm - 9:00 pm)

**Day 6: Ukhrul to Moreh**

* **Morning:** Drive to Moreh (8:00 am - 10:00 am), located on the India-Myanmar border
* **Afternoon:** Explore the town, visit the border gate, and see the local market (11:00 am - 2:00 pm)
* **Evening:** Stay overnight at a local guesthouse in Moreh (5:00 pm - onwards)

* **Afternoon:** Drive back to Imphal (11:00 am - 1:00 pm) and spend some time shopping for local handicrafts or visiting any last-minute sights (2:00 pm - 4:00 pm)
