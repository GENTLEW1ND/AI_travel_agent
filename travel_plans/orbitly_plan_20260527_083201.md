## Itinerary
Based on your query, I've created a 7-day travel itinerary for a trip to Kolkata from Delhi. Since you're looking for affordable options, I've considered the hostel results provided.

**Day 1: Arrival in Kolkata**

* Flight from Delhi to Kolkata (assuming a morning flight)
* Check-in at Bohemian Backpackers Kolkata (approximately $7/night), which is close to the heart of Kolkata
* Visit the nearby Indian Museum (entry fee: ₹ 20) to get a glimpse of India's rich history and culture
* Explore the surrounding area, including the famous College Street and its many bookstores

**Day 2: Kolkata City Tour**

* Start the day with a visit to the Victoria Memorial (entry fee: ₹ 20), a stunning example of British architecture
* Take a stroll along the Maidan, a large open space in the heart of the city
* Visit the St. Paul's Cathedral (entry fee: free), a beautiful Anglican cathedral
* Try some traditional Bengali cuisine for lunch at a local restaurant
* In the evening, take a walk along the Hooghly River and enjoy the sunset

**Day 3: Temple Tour**

* Visit the Dakshineswar Kali Temple (entry fee: free), a famous Hindu temple located on the banks of the Hooghly River
* Take a ferry ride to the Belur Math (entry fee: free), a beautiful temple complex founded by Swami Vivekananda
* Visit the Kalighat Kali Temple (entry fee: free), another famous Hindu temple in Kolkata
* Try some street food at the nearby Kalighat market

**Day 4: Cultural Experiences**

* Visit the Rabindra Sadan (entry fee: free), a cultural complex dedicated to the life and works of Rabindranath Tagore
* Attend a performance at the Academy of Fine Arts (entry fee: varies), which hosts various cultural events and exhibitions
* Explore the nearby Camac Street, known for its trendy cafes and shops
* Try some traditional Bengali sweets at a local shop

**Day 5: Day Trip to Sundarbans**

* Take a day trip to the Sundarbans (approximately ₹ 1,500 per person, including transportation and lunch), the largest mangrove forest in the world
* Explore the forest and its wildlife, including the Royal Bengal Tiger
* Return to Kolkata in the evening and relax at the hostel

**Day 6: Shopping and Food**

* Spend the day shopping at the New Market (entry fee: free), a historic marketplace in Kolkata
* Try some street food at the market, including the famous Kolkata-style biryani
* Visit the nearby Oberoi Grand Hotel (entry fee: free), a luxurious hotel with a beautiful courtyard
* In the evening, enjoy a traditional Bengali dinner at a local restaurant

**Day 7: Departure**

* Spend the morning at leisure, exploring the surrounding area or last-minute shopping
* Check-out of the hostel and depart for the airport (approximately ₹ 200-300 by taxi)
* Flight from Kolkata to Delhi (assuming an afternoon flight)
