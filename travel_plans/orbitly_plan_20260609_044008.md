* Check-in at one of the recommended hostels:

* Visit the famous Ram Jhula and Lakshman Jhula in the evening

**Day 2: Rishikesh**

* Start the day with a yoga session at one of the many yoga centers in Rishikesh
* Visit the Parmarth Niketan Ashram, which is one of the largest ashrams in Rishikesh
* Take a dip in the holy Ganges River at the Triveni Ghat
* Explore the Rishikesh market and try some local cuisine, such as vegetarian thalis or Ayurvedic food
* Watch the Ganga Aarti at the Parmarth Niketan Ashram in the evening

**Day 3: Rishikesh**

* Visit the Beatles Ashram, which is a popular spot for music lovers
* Explore the Rishikesh market and try some local handicrafts, such as wooden artifacts or handmade jewelry
* Enjoy a sunset view of the Ganges River at the Ram Jhula

**Day 4: Rishikesh to Haridwar**

* Take a taxi or bus from Rishikesh to Haridwar (approximately 1 hour journey)
* Visit the famous Har Ki Pauri Ghat, which is a sacred spot for Hindus
* Take a dip in the holy Ganges River at the Har Ki Pauri Ghat
* Explore the Haridwar market and try some local street food, such as kachoris or chaat items
* Watch the Ganga Aarti at the Har Ki Pauri Ghat in the evening

**Day 5: Haridwar**

* Visit the Chandi Devi Temple, which is a popular spot for trekking and offers stunning views of the surrounding hills
* Take a cable car ride to the top of the hill for a panoramic view of Haridwar
* Visit the Mansa Devi Temple, which is another popular spot for trekking and offers stunning views of the surrounding hills
* Explore the Haridwar market and try some local handicrafts, such as wooden artifacts or handmade jewelry

**Day 6: Haridwar to Mussoorie**

* Take a taxi or bus from Haridwar to Mussoorie (approximately 2 hours journey)
* Visit the famous Mall Road, which is a popular spot for shopping and dining
* Explore the Mussoorie market and try some local cuisine, such as Tibetan food or bakeries
* Visit the Gun Hill, which offers stunning views of the surrounding hills and valleys

**Day 7: Mussoorie**

* Visit the Kempty Falls, which is a popular spot for trekking and offers stunning views of the surrounding hills
* Take a hike to the top of the hill for a panoramic view of Mussoorie
* Visit the Company Garden, which is a popular spot for picnics and offers stunning views of the surrounding hills
* Depart from Mussoorie to Dehradun Airport (DED) for your onward journey
