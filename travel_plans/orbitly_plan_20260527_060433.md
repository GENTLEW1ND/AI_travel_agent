## Itinerary
It appears that the provided flight results are not relevant to planning a 7-day trip to India, as none of the flights listed are going to India. However, I can still provide you with a suggested itinerary for a 7-day trip to India.

**Day 1: Arrival in New Delhi**

* Arrive at Indira Gandhi International Airport in New Delhi
* Check-in to a hostel in the city, such as Joey's Hostel, which is located near the train station and has a lively atmosphere
* Spend the day exploring the city, visiting famous landmarks such as the Red Fort, Qutub Minar, and India Gate
* In the evening, try some of the local street food and explore the nightlife of the city

**Day 2: New Delhi**

* Start the day with a visit to the Akshardham Temple, a beautiful and peaceful place to learn about Hinduism and Indian culture
* Next, visit the National Museum, which has a vast collection of artifacts and exhibits on Indian history and culture
* In the afternoon, take a stroll through the Lodi Gardens, a beautiful park with gardens, lakes, and walking trails
* In the evening, enjoy a traditional Indian dinner and watch a cultural performance, such as a dance or music show

**Day 3: Agra**

* Take a day trip to Agra, which is about 3 hours away from New Delhi by car or train
* Visit the Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the World and a symbol of love and beauty
* Next, visit the Agra Fort, a beautiful and historic fort that was once the residence of the Mughal emperors
* Return to New Delhi in the evening and enjoy a relaxing dinner at a local restaurant

**Day 4: Jaipur**

* Take a flight or train to Jaipur, which is about 5 hours away from New Delhi
* Check-in to a hostel in the city and spend the day exploring the city, visiting famous landmarks such as the Amber Fort, Hawa Mahal, and City Palace
* In the evening, take a stroll through the bustling streets of the city and try some of the local street food

**Day 5: Jaipur**

* Start the day with a visit to the Nahargarh Fort, which offers stunning views of the city
* Next, visit the Jal Mahal, a beautiful palace that is situated in the middle of a lake
* In the afternoon, take a cooking class and learn how to make some of the local dishes, such as dal makhani and naan bread
* In the evening, enjoy a relaxing dinner at a local restaurant and watch a cultural performance

**Day 6: Udaipur**

* Take a flight or train to Udaipur, which is about 7 hours away from Jaipur
* Check-in to a hostel in the city and spend the day exploring the city, visiting famous landmarks such as the City Palace, Lake Pichola, and Jag Mandir
* In the evening, take a boat ride on the lake and enjoy the stunning views of the city

**Day 7: Departure from Udaipur**

* Spend the morning shopping for souvenirs or exploring the city
* Depart for the airport and fly back home

Hotel Suggestions:

* Zostel Jaipur in Jaipur
* Moustache Udaipur in Udaipur
