**Updated Itinerary:**

**Trip Overview:**
Our 10-day journey will take us from Kolkata to Delhi, exploring the vibrant city's rich history, culture, and landmarks. We will then travel to Rishikesh, a spiritual and adventure hub, and experience the best of its hospitality, cuisine, and entertainment.

**Flights:**
Although the original flight information did not include a direct flight from Kolkata to Delhi, we recommend booking a flight with a reliable airline such as IndiGo, SpiceJet, or Air India. For the journey from Delhi to Rishikesh, we can take a domestic flight or a train, depending on the availability and preference.

**Hotel Accommodations:**
We suggest staying at a hotel in Central Delhi, such as:

* The Lalit New Delhi (8.2/10)
* The Connaught, New Delhi- Ihcl SeleQtions (9.7/10)
* The Taj Mahal Hotel

For Rishikesh, we recommend the following hotels:

* Hotel Ganga Kinare
* Moustache Rishikesh Luxuria
* Arogyadham Wellness

**Itinerary:**

**Day 1: Arrival in Delhi**

* Arrive at Delhi airport and check-in at our hotel
* Spend the day exploring the city, visiting famous landmarks like the Red Fort, Qutub Minar, and India Gate
* Enjoy a traditional Delhi dinner at a local eatery

**Day 2: Delhi Sightseeing**

* Start the day with a delicious breakfast at a local restaurant
* Visit the Akshardham Temple, a stunning example of Indian architecture
* Explore the Chandni Chowk market, one of the oldest and busiest markets in Delhi
* Enjoy a traditional Delhi street food dinner at a local eatery

**Day 3: Delhi Sightseeing**

* Visit the Lotus Temple, a beautiful Bahai temple
* Explore the National Museum, which houses a vast collection of Indian art and artifacts
* Take a stroll through the Lodi Gardens, a beautiful park with historic monuments
* Enjoy a cultural performance at a local theater or auditorium

**Day 4: Day Trip to Agra**

* Take a day trip to Agra, home to the Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the World
* Visit the Agra Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site
* Return to Delhi in the evening and enjoy a relaxing dinner at a local restaurant

**Day 5: Delhi Sightseeing**

* Visit the Hauz Khas Village, a charming neighborhood with historic monuments, trendy restaurants, and boutiques
* Explore the Crafts Museum, which showcases Indian handicrafts and textiles
* Take a cooking class and learn how to make traditional Indian dishes
* Enjoy a farewell dinner at a local restaurant

**Day 6: Travel to Rishikesh**

* Take a domestic flight or train from Delhi to Rishikesh
* Check-in at our hotel and spend the day exploring the city
* Visit the famous Ram Jhula and Lakshman Jhula bridges
* Enjoy a traditional Rishikesh dinner at a local eatery

**Day 7: Rishikesh Sightseeing**

* Start the day with a yoga session or meditation at a local ashram
* Visit the Parmarth Niketan ashram, a famous spiritual center
* Explore the Rishikesh market, which offers a variety of handicrafts and souvenirs
* Enjoy a relaxing dinner at a local restaurant

**Day 8: Rishikesh Adventure**

* Go rafting or kayaking in the Ganges River
* Take a trek to the nearby hills and enjoy the scenic views
* Visit the Neelkanth Mahadev Temple, a beautiful shrine dedicated to Lord Shiva
* Enjoy a campfire and stargazing in the evening

**Day 9: Rishikesh Sightseeing**

* Visit the Triveni Ghat, a sacred bathing spot
* Explore the Rishikesh Museum, which showcases the history and culture of the region
* Take a stroll along the Ganges River and enjoy the scenic views
* Enjoy a farewell dinner at a local restaurant

**Day 10: Departure from Rishikesh**

* Spend the morning at leisure or visit any last-minute attractions
* Check-out of the hotel and depart for the airport or train station
* Take a flight or train back to Kolkata, bringing back memories of our wonderful trip to Delhi and Rishikesh

**Additional Tips and Recommendations:**

* Book flights and hotels in advance to avoid peak season prices
* Try local cuisine and street food for an authentic experience
* Respect local customs and traditions when visiting temples and monuments
* Stay hydrated and take breaks in the heat
* Enjoy the vibrant cities and make the most of our 10-day journey!
