## Itinerary
Given the intent is NEW_TRIP, I will create a completely new 10-day itinerary for a trip to Manipur from Assam.

### Day 1: Arrival in Imphal
- **Morning:** Arrive at Imphal Airport. Check into a hotel like Classic Grande Imphal - A Member of Radisson Individuals or Aurora Boutique Hotel for a comfortable stay.
- **Afternoon:** Visit the Kangla Fort, a historic fort that stands as a symbol of Manipur's rich history.
- **Evening:** Explore the local market, such as the Ima Keithel (Mothers' Market), to experience the local culture and cuisine.

### Day 2: Imphal
- **Morning:** Visit the Shaheed Minar and the Manipur State Museum to understand the state's history and culture better.
- **Afternoon:** Take a trip to the Loktak Lake, the largest freshwater lake in northeastern India, known for its floating islands.
- **Evening:** Enjoy a traditional Manipuri dinner and watch a cultural performance.

### Day 3: Imphal to Bishnupur
- **Morning:** Drive to Bishnupur, known for its ancient temples.
- **Afternoon:** Visit the Vishnu Temple and other historical sites in Bishnupur.
- **Evening:** Return to Imphal and relax at the hotel.

### Day 4: Ukhrul
- **Morning:** Drive to Ukhrul, a picturesque hill station known for its natural beauty.
- **Afternoon:** Explore the town, visit the Ukhrul District Museum, and enjoy the scenic views.
- **Evening:** Overnight stay in Ukhrul.

### Day 5: Ukhrul
- **Morning:** Trek to the Khangkhui Lime Caves, a fascinating natural wonder.
- **Afternoon:** Continue exploring Ukhrul, visiting the Nillai Tea Estate, and enjoying the local scenery.
- **Evening:** Return to Imphal.

### Day 6: Andro
- **Morning:** Visit the Andro village, known for its pottery and traditional crafts.
- **Afternoon:** Explore the Mutua Museum and learn about the local crafts and culture.
- **Evening:** Back to Imphal.

### Day 7: Thoubal
- **Morning:** Drive to Thoubal, a district known for its natural beauty and historical significance.
- **Afternoon:** Visit the Thoubal Bazar and explore the local culture.
- **Evening:** Return to Imphal.

### Day 8: Churachandpur
- **Morning:** Drive to Churachandpur, a town with a mix of tribal cultures.
- **Afternoon:** Explore the town, visit the Tuibuong Tribal Museum, and learn about the local tribes.
- **Evening:** Overnight stay in Churachandpur.

### Day 9: Churachandpur to Imphal
- **Morning:** Return to Imphal.
- **Afternoon:** Spend the day at leisure, perhaps doing some last-minute shopping for souvenirs.
- **Evening:** Farewell dinner in Imphal.

### Day 10: Departure
- **Morning:** Spend the morning at leisure.
- **Afternoon:** Check out from the hotel and proceed to the airport for your departure flight.

This itinerary provides a comprehensive experience of Manipur, covering its history, culture, natural beauty, and local traditions. Enjoy your trip to Manipur!
