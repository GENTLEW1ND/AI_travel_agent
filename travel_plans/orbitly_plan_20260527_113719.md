* Spend the rest of the day exploring the charming Montmartre neighborhood, visiting the Sacré-Cœur Basilica, and enjoying the street performers and artists

**Day 2: Parisian Landmarks**

* Start the day with a private guided tour of the iconic Eiffel Tower
* Visit the nearby Champ de Mars park and enjoy the stunning views of the tower
* Head to the Louvre Museum, one of the world's largest and most famous museums, and explore the impressive collection of art and artifacts
* In the evening, enjoy a Seine River cruise and admire the city's landmarks while sipping champagne

**Day 3: Royal Palaces and Gardens**

* Visit the Palace of Versailles, a former royal residence with stunning gardens and fountain shows (book a private guided tour to avoid the crowds)
* Explore the Hall of Mirrors, the Royal Chapel, and the Royal Opera
* Return to Paris and visit the Jardin des Tuileries, a beautiful garden with fountains, statues, and walking paths
* In the evening, enjoy a dinner at a luxurious restaurant, such as Le Bristol or Le Royal Monceau

**Day 4: Montmartre and Pigalle**

* Spend the day exploring the historic neighborhood of Montmartre, visiting the Place du Tertre, and admiring the street artists and performers
* Visit the Musée de Montmartre, which showcases the history and art of the neighborhood
* Head to the nearby Pigalle neighborhood, known for its nightlife, cabarets, and jazz clubs
* In the evening, enjoy a dinner and show at the world-famous Moulin Rouge

**Day 5: Luxury Shopping and Spa**

* Spend the day shopping at the luxurious department stores, such as Galeries Lafayette and Le Bon Marché
* Visit the iconic Chanel flagship store on the Rue Cambon
* Treat yourself to a rejuvenating spa day at a luxurious hotel, such as the Ritz Paris or the Four Seasons Hotel George V
* In the evening, enjoy a cocktail at a rooftop bar, such as the Bar Hemingway at the Ritz Paris

**Day 6: Impressionist Art and Gardens**

* Visit the Musée Marmottan Monet, which houses an impressive collection of Impressionist art
* Explore the beautiful gardens of the Musée Rodin, which features sculptures by Auguste Rodin
* Head to the Musée d'Orsay, which showcases an impressive collection of Impressionist and Post-Impressionist art
* In the evening, enjoy a dinner at a restaurant with a focus on seasonal, local ingredients, such as Le Comptoir du Relais

**Day 7: Departure**
