## Itinerary
Given the intent is NEW_TRIP, I will create a completely new itinerary for a 7-day trip to Manipur from Assam.

### Day 1: Arrival in Imphal, Manipur
- **Morning:** Arrive at Imphal International Airport. Check into your hotel and freshen up.
- **Afternoon:** Visit the Kangla Fort, a historic site that served as the palace of the Manipur rulers. 
- **Evening:** Explore the local market, especially the Ima Keithel (Mother's Market), which is run entirely by women.

### Day 2: Imphal
- **Morning:** Visit the Loktak Lake, the largest freshwater lake in Northeast India, known for its floating islands (phumdis). Take a boat ride to the Sendra Island for a panoramic view of the lake.
- **Afternoon:** Visit the Keibul Lamjao National Park, the only floating national park in the world, located on the Loktak Lake. It is home to the endangered Manipur brow-antlered deer (Sangai).
- **Evening:** Return to Imphal and enjoy local cuisine for dinner.

### Day 3: Imphal to Ukhrul
- **Morning:** Drive to Ukhrul, about 84 km from Imphal. On the way, stop at the Khayang Peak for a breathtaking view.
- **Afternoon:** Visit the Shirui Lily Park, home to the rare and endangered Shirui Lily, which blooms in the months of May and June.
- **Evening:** Stay overnight in Ukhrul.

### Day 4: Ukhrul
- **Morning:** Explore the village of Ukhrul. Visit the Hundung Mangva Cave, a limestone cave.
- **Afternoon:** Trek to the Khangkhui Cave, known for its stunning natural beauty and historical significance.
- **Evening:** Enjoy the local Tangkhul cuisine.

### Day 5: Ukhrul to Moreh
- **Morning:** Drive to Moreh, located on the India-Myanmar border. This is about 110 km from Ukhrul.
- **Afternoon:** Explore the town, visit the border gate, and see the local market.
- **Evening:** Stay overnight in Moreh.

### Day 6: Moreh
- **Morning:** Visit the Shangshak village, known for its traditional houses and scenic views.
- **Afternoon:** Explore the local handicrafts and buy some souvenirs.
- **Evening:** Prepare for your departure the next day.

### Day 7: Departure
- **Morning:** Drive back to Imphal (about 110 km) and spend some time shopping for local handicrafts or visiting any last-minute sights.
- **Afternoon:** Depart from Imphal International Airport for your return journey.

### Accommodation:
Given the hotel information, consider staying in affordable backpacker hostels or homestays in Imphal, Ukhrul, and Moreh. These options are not only budget-friendly but also provide a chance to experience the local culture closely. Some recommended places include:
- In Imphal: Look for homestays near the city center for easy access to major attractions.
- In Ukhrul and Moreh: Opt for local guesthouses or homestays that offer a glimpse into the traditional Tangkhul and local lifestyles.

### Transportation:
- For traveling between cities like Imphal, Ukhrul, and Moreh, consider hiring a car or using local transportation services. The roads can be scenic but may require patience due to the terrains.

### Activities and Experiences:
- Trekking in Ukhrul to explore its natural beauty.
- Boating in Loktak Lake.
- Visiting the floating gardens of Loktak Lake.
- Exploring the local markets and trying the local cuisine in each place.

This itinerary provides a mix of culture, nature, and adventure, giving you a comprehensive experience of Manipur. Enjoy your trip!
