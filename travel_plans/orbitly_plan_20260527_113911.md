* Enjoy dinner at a modern French restaurant, such as Le Grand Colbert or Le Petit Châtelet

**Day 6: Day Trip to Reims**

* Enjoy a champagne tasting at a local vineyard, such as Veuve Clicquot or Taittinger (Ticket price: around €50 per person)
* Return to Paris in the evening and enjoy a farewell dinner at a Michelin-starred restaurant, such as Le Bristol or Le Plaza Athénée

**Day 7: Departure**

* Spend the morning shopping for souvenirs or visiting any last-minute attractions
* Depart for the airport and your return flight home
