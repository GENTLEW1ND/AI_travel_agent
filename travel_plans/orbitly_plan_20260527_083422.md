## Itinerary
Based on your query, I've created a 7-day travel itinerary for a trip to Kerala from Delhi. Since I don't have your specific flight details, I'll provide a general outline of the trip. Please note that you can adjust the itinerary according to your interests, budget, and preferences.

**Day 1: Delhi to Cochin**

* Arrive at Cochin International Airport
* Check-in to your hotel in Fort Kochi (you can choose a backpacker hostel or a budget hotel)
* Visit the Fort Kochi area, including the Dutch Palace, St. Francis Church, and the Chinese Fishing Nets
* Enjoy a traditional Kerala dinner and explore the local markets

**Day 2: Cochin to Munnar**

* Drive to Munnar (approximately 4 hours)
* Check-in to your hotel in Munnar
* Visit the Tea Museum, Mattupetty Dam, and Echo Point
* Explore the Munnar town and enjoy the scenic views of the tea plantations

**Day 3: Munnar**

* Visit the Rajamalai National Park to see the Nilgiri Tahr
* Trek to the Top Station for breathtaking views of the Western Ghats
* Visit the Blossom International Park and the Pothanmedu Viewpoint
* Enjoy a campfire and stargazing in the evening

**Day 4: Munnar to Alleppey**

* Drive to Alleppey (approximately 4 hours)
* Check-in to your houseboat
* Cruise through the backwaters of Alleppey and enjoy the scenic views
* Visit the local villages and enjoy the traditional Kerala cuisine

**Day 5: Alleppey**

* Continue the houseboat cruise and explore the backwaters
* Visit the Kuttanad region and see the paddy fields
* Enjoy the sunset cruise and the local music and dance performances

**Day 6: Alleppey to Kovalam**

* Drive to Kovalam (approximately 4 hours)
* Check-in to your hotel in Kovalam
* Visit the Kovalam Beach and enjoy the sunset
* Explore the local markets and try the seafood

**Day 7: Kovalam to Trivandrum and Departure**

* Visit the Padmanabhaswamy Temple and the Trivandrum Museum
* Drive to the Trivandrum International Airport for your departure flight

Hotel Suggestions:

* Backpacker hostels: Hippie In Heels, Zostel, or Munnar Tea Valley Resort
* Budget hotels: Hotel Fort Kochi, Hotel Munnar Inn, or Hotel Kovalam Beach

 Transportation:

* You can hire a taxi or a driver for the entire trip
* Alternatively, you can use public transportation, such as buses and trains

Activities:

* Houseboat cruise in Alleppey
* Trekking in Munnar
* Visiting the local markets and trying the street food
* Enjoying the scenic views and the sunset

Budget:

* Accommodation: ₹1,500 - ₹3,000 per night (approximately $20-$40 USD)
* Transportation: ₹2,000 - ₹5,000 (approximately $25-$65 USD) for the entire trip
* Food: ₹500 - ₹1,000 per day (approximately $7-$15 USD)
* Activities: ₹1,000 - ₹2,000 (approximately $15-$25 USD) per person

Total estimated budget for the 7-day trip: ₹25,000 - ₹50,000 (approximately $350-$650 USD) per person

Please note that the budget is an estimate and can vary depending on your preferences and exchange rates. Additionally, you can adjust the itinerary to fit your interests and budget. Have a wonderful trip to Kerala!
