## Itinerary
Given the information, I'll create a 7-day travel itinerary for a trip to Kolkata from Delhi. Since the flight details are not provided, I'll assume you have booked a suitable flight from Delhi to Kolkata.

**Day 1: Arrival in Kolkata**

* Arrive at Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose International Airport (CCU)
* Take a taxi or metro to your hotel (approximately 30-40 minutes)
* Check-in at one of the recommended hostels:
	+ Bohemian Backpackers Kolkata (around $7/night)
	+ Bubble Beds Hostel ( prices vary)
	+ Suitcase Stories (around $4/night for solo travelers)
* Spend the day exploring the nearby area, such as Park Street or Esplanade
* Try some local street food, like jhal muri or mishti doi

**Day 2: Kolkata City Tour**

* Start the day with a visit to the Victoria Memorial (entry fee: ₹20)
* Explore the Indian Museum (entry fee: ₹20)
* Walk to the nearby St. Paul's Cathedral (free entry)
* Visit the New Market (also known as Hogg Market) for some shopping and street food
* In the evening, take a stroll along the Hooghly River and enjoy the sunset

**Day 3: Cultural Experiences**

* Visit the Dakshineswar Kali Temple (free entry, but donations welcome)
* Take a ferry ride to the Belur Math (entry fee: ₹10)
* Explore the Kalighat Temple (free entry, but donations welcome)
* Attend a traditional Bengali dance or music performance at a local cultural center

**Day 4: Food and Markets**

* Visit the College Street Market for some book shopping and street food
* Explore the Gariahat Market for some local handicrafts and souvenirs
* Try some traditional Bengali cuisine at a local restaurant, such as fish fry or biryani
* Visit the Kumortuli Park to see the potters at work

**Day 5: Day Trip to the Sundarbans**

* Take a day trip to the Sundarbans National Park (approximately 3 hours away)
* Explore the mangrove forests and spot wildlife, such as tigers or crocodiles
* Enjoy a boat ride through the park's waterways
* Return to Kolkata in the evening

**Day 6: More Kolkata Exploration**

* Visit the Science City (entry fee: ₹50)
* Explore the Nicco Park (entry fee: ₹150)
* Visit the Birla Planetarium (entry fee: ₹50)
* Take a walk through the Maidan, a large open park in the heart of the city

**Day 7: Departure**

* Spend the morning shopping for souvenirs or visiting any last-minute attractions
* Check-out of your hotel and head to the airport for your return flight to Delhi
