## Itinerary
Based on the provided information, I'll create a 7-day travel itinerary for a trip to Shillong from Delhi.

**Day 1: Delhi to Shillong**

* Since the flight results are unknown, I recommend booking a flight from Delhi to Shillong (the closest airport is Umroi Airport, but most flights land at Guwahati International Airport, which is about 100 km from Shillong).
* Upon arrival, take a taxi or bus to Shillong (approximately 3-4 hours from Guwahati).
* Check-in at a mid-range hotel like Highwinds Hotel or Aiban's Guest House.
* Spend the rest of the day exploring Shillong's local market and getting familiar with the city.

**Day 2: Shillong**

* Start the day with a visit to Ward's Lake, a beautiful man-made lake with gardens and walking trails.
* Next, head to the Shillong Golf Course, one of the oldest golf courses in India.
* Visit the Don Bosco Museum, which showcases the culture and history of the region.
* In the evening, explore the Police Bazaar, a popular shopping and dining area.

**Day 3: Shillong to Cherrapunjee**

* Take a day trip to Cherrapunjee (approximately 2 hours from Shillong), known for being one of the wettest places on Earth.
* Visit the Nohkalikai Falls, the tallest plunge waterfall in India.
* Explore the Mawsmai Cave, a stunning limestone cave.
* Return to Shillong in the evening and enjoy dinner at a local restaurant.

**Day 4: Shillong to Mawlynnong**

* Take a day trip to Mawlynnong (approximately 3 hours from Shillong), known for being Asia's cleanest village.
* Visit the Living Root Bridge, a natural bridge made from the roots of trees.
* Explore the village and see the local way of life.
* Return to Shillong in the evening and relax at your hotel.

**Day 5: Shillong**

* Spend the day exploring Shillong's surroundings, such as the Elephant Falls and the Shillong Peak.
* Visit the Air Force Museum, which showcases the history of the Indian Air Force.
* In the evening, enjoy a traditional Khasi dinner and watch a local cultural performance.

**Day 6: Shillong**

* Take a day trip to the nearby Umiam Lake (approximately 1 hour from Shillong), a beautiful man-made lake with boating and water sports facilities.
* Visit the nearby Lum Nehru Park, a beautiful park with gardens and walking trails.
* Return to Shillong in the evening and shop for souvenirs.

**Day 7: Shillong to Delhi**

* Spend the morning at leisure, exploring Shillong's local market or visiting any last-minute attractions.
* Depart for the airport (Guwahati or Umroi) and take a flight back to Delhi.
