* Taj Palaces

* Joyous Homestay (for a mid-range option)
* Gostops Plus Rishikesh
* Shiv Shakti Hostel
* Live Free Hostel Rishikesh
* Moustache Rishikesh Luxuria
* Arogyadham Wellness

**Itinerary:**

**Day 1: Arrival in Delhi**

* Arrive at Delhi airport
* Check-in to a hotel in Delhi (e.g., Oberoi Resorts or Leela Hotels)
* Spend the day exploring the city, visiting famous landmarks like the Red Fort, Qutub Minar, or India Gate

**Day 2: Delhi Sightseeing**

* Visit the Akshardham Temple, a stunning example of Indian architecture
* Explore the Chandni Chowk market, one of the oldest and busiest markets in India
* Enjoy a traditional Indian dinner at a local restaurant

**Day 3: Delhi to Agra**

* Take a day trip to Agra, home to the Taj Mahal (approximately 3-4 hours by car or train)
* Visit the Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the World
* Return to Delhi in the evening and spend the night at the hotel

**Day 4: Delhi to Jaipur**

* Take a flight or train to Jaipur, the Pink City (approximately 1-2 hours by flight or 4-5 hours by train)
* Check-in to a hotel in Jaipur (e.g., Taj Palaces or Leela Hotels)
* Spend the day exploring the city, visiting famous landmarks like the Amber Fort, City Palace, or Hawa Mahal

**Day 5: Jaipur Sightseeing**

* Visit the Jantar Mantar, an ancient astronomical observatory
* Explore the Johri Bazaar, a famous market for jewelry and handicrafts
* Enjoy a traditional Rajasthani dinner at a local restaurant

**Day 6: Jaipur to Rishikesh**

* Take a flight or train to Rishikesh (approximately 1-2 hours by flight or 6-7 hours by train)
* Check-in to a hotel in Rishikesh (e.g., Gostops Plus Rishikesh or Shiv Shakti Hostel)
* Spend the day exploring the city, visiting famous landmarks like the Ram Jhula, Lakshman Jhula, or Parmarth Niketan

**Day 7: Rishikesh Sightseeing**

* Visit the Triveni Ghat, a sacred spot for Hindus
* Explore the Beatles Ashram, a famous spot for meditation and yoga
* Enjoy a traditional Indian dinner at a local restaurant

**Day 8: Rishikesh Adventure**

* Go rafting or kayaking in the Ganges River
* Visit the Neer Garh Waterfall, a beautiful spot for trekking and relaxation
* Enjoy a campfire and stargazing in the evening

**Day 9: Departure from Rishikesh**

* Check-out of the hotel and depart for the airport or railway station
* Return to Shillong by flight (please book a suitable flight from Rishikesh to Shillong)
